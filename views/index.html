Simple Scheduling Over Email

Create a private email list which keeps track of when people are available.

Start Scheduling

How does it work?

1. Send an email to start@convenely.com

Include a subject and message telling everyone what you're arranging. After the message list the email addresses of everyone to include on the mailing list.

2. Reply with when you're available.

We create a private mailing list for your event and mail everyone with your message. As people reply with their availability we keep track and include it in a summary on every message.

3. Everyone sees the whole picture.

It's that simple! A private email list that keeps track of when everybody is free. And since it's all done over email you can use it from any device, anywhere.

Anything else I should know?

Add someone to the event

Send an email to your list with the subject Add and list the email addresses of people you want to add.

Remove someone from the event

Send an email to your list with the subject Remove and list the email addresses of people you want to remove from the list.

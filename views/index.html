Start Scheduling

How does it work?

1. Send an email to start@convenely.com

A single email is all it takes to start arranging your event. Include a subject and message describing the event then list the email addresses of everyone you want to invite.

2. Reply with when you're available.

We create a private email list for your event and mail everyone about it. As people reply with their availability we keep track and include it in every message.

3. Everyone sees the whole picture.

It's that simple! A private email list that keeps track of when everybody is free. And since it's all done over email you can use it from anywhere on any device.

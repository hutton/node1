Start Scheduling

How does it work?

1. Send an email to start@convenely.com

Include a subject and message letting everyone know what you're arranging. After the message list the email addresses of everyone to include on the mailing list.

2. Reply with when you're available.

Convenely creates a private mailing list for your event and mails everyone with your message. As people reply with their availability we keep track and include it in a summary in every message.

3. Everyone sees the whole picture.

It's that simple! A private email list that keeps track of when everybody is free. And since it's all done over email you can use it from any device, anywhere.

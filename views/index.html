Start Scheduling

How does it work?

1. Send an email to start@convenely.com.

Include a subject and message then list the email addresses of people you'd like to invite.

2. Convenely creates a private mailing list and sends the message to everyone who's invited.

3. As people reply with when they're free we keep track and include it in every message.

  • "I can do any Tuesday or Wednesday."
  • "Any day next week is good for me."
  • "I can only do the 27th or 1st."

4. Easy. And it's all done through email from any device!

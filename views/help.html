So it's a group email list, right?

Convenely gives you an email list with a difference. We use Natural Language Processing (NLP) to extract availability information from emails sent to the list and use it to show who is available when. This makes finding the best time to do something with a group of people a breeze and because it's all done over email you can do it from any device anywhere.

It couldn't be simpler, there's no link to send around or website to visit just reply to the email list and we'll do the rest.

When I reply to the list who sees my response?

Everyone on the list sees your response, it works just like a regular mailing list.

Do you keep messages?

No, our algorithums read every email to extact availability information then they're forgotten.

How private is the list?

Only people on a list can send messages to it. Everyone on the list will see every other email address though, these are displayed in the availability calendar.

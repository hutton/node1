Getting Started


1. Select when you're free.

Click the days when you're free and see who else is available.

2. Invite others.

Click the envelope and select 'Invite someone' to send an email inviting people to the event.

3. Find the best date.

See the top three dates for your event when you open it up or click the event title.

4. Contact everyone.

Every event has it's own private email group, click the button to send an email to everyone participating in the event.



FAQ


No passwords, how does that work?

When you join an event every email you receieve for that event has your own unique 'View event' link, click this and we know it's you.

How do I add people to an event?

Every event has a link for people who want to join it. We email you the link when you join an event, you can also get it from the settings page or from the envelope on the event page.

Can anyone add people to an event?

Anyone who is part of an event can add people to it.

How do I remove people from an event?

You can remove anyone from an event in the settings page or remove yourself by clicking 'Unsubscribe' at the bottom of any email.

How private is the event email list?

Only people on a list can send messages to it. Everyone on the list will see who else is on the list.
